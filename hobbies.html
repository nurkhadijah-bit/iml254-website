Reasons why I love Drawing

🖌 I always admired people that can draw. For me, drawing is something that I find comfort in. Every time I am stressed, I doodle or scribble to mute the thoughts in my head.

🖌 I picked up this hobby during the lockdown when I was really into anime. I was fascinated by how the animation, art style, and storylines came together to create something beautiful in art.

🖌 This curiosity about how anime was made led me to fall in love with art in the first place.
